## Summary
Han's information-vs-narrative distinction: **information** is additive, novelty-driven, fleeting ("they no longer inform — they deform"); **narrative** is synthesizing, binding past/present/future into *orientation* via "the long, slow, lingering gaze." Storytelling degrades into "storyselling."
