## When to Extract Quotes

- During **claim verification** — when you confirm a source says what the registry claims
- When a source uses **precise language** that would be weakened by paraphrasing
- When a finding includes **exact numbers, effect sizes, or statistical results**
- When an author states a position that your paper **agrees with or responds to**
- When a quote will serve as **grounds for an ARGUMENT** in the Discussion

## How to Cite Page Numbers

- **Journal articles:** use the published page number (p. 636) or section heading if no page numbers
- **Tables/Figures:** cite as (Table 2) or (Fig. 3, p. 12)
- **Online-only / preprints:** use section number or paragraph count from start of section
- **Guidelines / standards:** use section and clause numbers (Section 4.2, Clause 7.1)
